Easy No-Knead Bread

Classic easy bread recipe you can throw together in a couple of hours.

ingredients

  • 525g strong bread flour
  • 7g quick acting yeast
  • 15g olive oil
  • 450ml warm water
  • 12g fine sea salt

preparation

  1. Mix together all the ingredients with a spoon until combined into a sticky dough.
  2. Cover with cling film and leave in a warm spot for 2-4 hours.
  3. Heat the oven to 220c, and place a large casserole dish or dutch oven inside to pre-heat.
  4. Once the dough has risen, assemble the following in the pre-heated dutch oven:
    • 4-5 ice cubes (these create steam and a lovely crispy outer crust)
    • A large sheet of baking parchment to contain the dough
    • The freshly risen dough
    • A little sprinkle of flour (the dough may be quite wet!)
  5. Place the lid onto the dutch oven and put in the oven for 30-40 mins.
  6. The bread is ready once its golden brown on the outside, and givens a nice hollow sound when tapped.
  7. Remove from the baking paper, and place on a wire rack to cool before eating with LOTS OF BUTTER.
  8. Enjoy!
